Summary:

  • align-items: Aligns all items along the cross axis of the container. For example, align-items: center; centers all items vertically in a row layout.
  • align-self: Aligns individual items along the cross axis, overriding align-items for that specific item.
  • The values for both align-items and align-self include:
    • flex-start: Aligns to the start of the cross axis.
    • flex-end: Aligns to the end of the cross axis.
    • center: Centers the items along the cross axis.
    • stretch: Stretches the items to fill the container (default).
    • auto: Uses the value of align-items from the container.
